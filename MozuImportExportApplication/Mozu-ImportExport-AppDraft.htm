Install the Mozu Import Export Application on Your Tenant

After purchasing the Mozu Import Export Application, call your sales or professional services representative and request installation on your tenant. Mozu staff will install the app to your tenant, making it available for further configuration and usage in your storefront.

Configuration Requirements

To configure the Mozu Import Export Application, you must have the following:

  • The Mozu Import Export Application installed on your tenant.
  • An active SumoLogic account.
  • Access to modify and update your storefront theme.

Configure the Application

To integrate your PowerReview services with Mozu, enter your PowerReviews account information in your Mozu settings.

Configure PowerReviews Account Settings

  1. Log in to your Sumo Logic account.

Enable the App

After configuration, enable the Mozu Import Export Application in Mozu:

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings»Applications.
