Welcome to PowerReviews and Mozu

PowerReviews provides ratings and reviews that help increase buyer confidence and stimulate sales for your products. With PowerReviews, customers can leave ratings, write reviews, and ask questions directly on your product pages. PowerReviews is also capable of syndicating product reviews from all major retailers and search engines, increasing the reach of the reviews on your storefront.

The Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application seamlessly integrates your PowerReviews account with your storefront so that you can start displaying and generating reviews on your product and category pages. With the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application, you can import PowerReviews content on demand or at scheduled intervals between 1 to 12 hours, ensuring that your storefront is kept up to date with the latest available reviews.

Application Features:

  • Display PowerReviews ratings summaries, detailed reviews, Write a Review links, and Question and Answer boxes (Social Answers) using widgets in Site Builder.
  • Incorporate PowerReviews ratings into your storefront's faceting filters.
  • Import product reviews from PowerReviews to Mozu.
  • Export products and categories from your site to PowerReviews.
  • Export order history from your site to PowerReviews (to solicit revies in follow-up emails)
  • Send customer reviews received on your storefront to PowerReviews.
  • Schedule imports between PowerReviews and Mozu at set intervals or on demand.
  • Increase your site's search engine optimization using reviews on your product pages. Search engine bots use reviews content to index your page and bolster search results.
  • Place web beacon widgets on your checkout page to measure the impact that reviews and Q&A have on sale conversion.

Install the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application on Your Tenant

After purchasing the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application, call your sales or professional services representative and request installation on your tenant. Mozu staff will install the app to your tenant, making it available for further configuration and usage in your storefront.

Configure the Application

To integrate your PowerReviews services with Mozu, review the configuration requirements to ensure you have everything you need to be successful before you begin, and then complete the steps in this section.

Configuration Requirements

  • The Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application must be installed on your tenant.
  • You must have an active PowerReviews account.
  • You must have access to modify and update your site's theme.

Note: If you are switching from Volusion to Mozu and want to transfer your existing Volusion reviews to your Mozu site, contact our support team. If you are switching from a third-party site to Mozu and want to transfer your reviews, contact PowerReviews or an implementation engineer.

Obtain PowerReviews Account Credentials

Note your PowerReviews account credentials. You will enter these credentials in Mozu.

  1. Log in to your PowerReviews account.
  2. Click Configuration > Configure Reviews to display the general configuration settings for your PowerReviews account.
  3. Note the Merchant Group ID, Merchant ID, Site ID, FTP Site, FTP username, FTP password, and ZIP File Name.

Configuring PowerReviews to Automatically Receive Product and Order Information

One of the features of the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application is the ability to schedule recurring exports of product and order information from Mozu to PowerReviews. Scheduling these exports allows PowerReviews to utilize the latest version of your catalog and to gather feedback from shoppers about their shopping experience. For PowerReviews to pull the data that Mozu sends to it, enable automatic imports of product and order feeds in your PowerReviews account.

To configure PowerReviews to receive automatic imports of product feeds from Mozu:

  1. Contact PowerReviews support.
  2. Ask your PowerReviews support contact to configure your account to receive automatic product feeds on a daily basis.
  3. Mozu exports product information in a tab-delimited UTF-8 file with the following naming convention: products_MerchantGroupID_MerchantID.txt. You will need to provide this information to your support contact.

To configure PowerReviews to receive automatic imports of order feeds from Mozu:

  1. In your PowerReviews account, click Configuration > Configure Follow Up Email.
  2. Click Upload Orders to configure your order feed:
  3. Under Set Up Your Order Feed, select Get File From FTP and enable Automatically Import Daily.
  4. Enter the FTP URL, username, and password to match the values of your PowerReviews configuration.
  5. Click Next to test the order feed.
  6. On the following screen, enter the name of the order export feed. Mozu exports order information in a CSV file with the following naming convention: orders_MerchantGroupID_MerchantID.csv.

    Note: If you encounter issues during this process, contact PowerReviews support.

Configure the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application

  1. Log in to Mozu Admin.
  2. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  3. Click Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application.
  4. Click the Configuration link to open configuration settings.
  5. Open the Settings tab.
  6. Click Add Merchant Group to add your PowerReview account information to Mozu:
    1. Enter the Merchant Group ID.
    2. Enter the FTP Username.
    3. Enter the FTP Password.
    4. In the PowerReview Import Frequency drop-down, choose a frequency for importing content from PowerReviews to your Mozu site.
    5. Click Show Advanced Settings.
    6. Enter the FTP Location using the FTP site from your PowerReviews account.
    7. Enter the PowerReview Import Filename using the ZIP file name from your PowerReviews account.
    8. Enter a PowerReview Import Ready Filename. If your PowerReview Import Filename is pwr.zip, enter done.txt. If your PowerReview Import Filename is pwr-something.zip, enter done-something.txt. In this example, we enter done-locale2.txt because the PowerReviews Import Filename is pwr-locale2.zip.
  7. Click Add Mozu Site to configure PowerReviews for your storefront:
    1. Select your storefront from the Mozu Site drop-down.
    2. Enable Enable Product Export to send product information from your Mozu site to PowerReviews. This checkbox is required in order for PowerReviews to match reviews content with the products on your site.

      Note: For PowerReviews to pull in the product export sent by Mozu, configure your PowerReviews account to receive automatic product feeds.

    3. Enable Enable Order Export to send order history information to PowerReviews. This information helps PowerReviews to remind shoppers about reviewing recently purchased products, suggest other products shoppers may enjoy, and provide other similar benefits that you can configure within your PowerReviews account.

      Note: For PowerReviews to pull in the order export sent by Mozu, configure your PowerReviews account to receive automatic order feeds.

    4. Enter the Merchant ID.
    5. Enter the Merchant Site ID.
    6. Enter the Locale to match your site settings.
    7. Choose how much data to export using the Number of days to include in order export.
  8. Click Save to save changes to the Mozu Site configuration.
  9. Click Save to save changes to the Merchant Group configuration.

Enable the App

After configuration, enable the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application in Mozu and extend the PowerReviews theme to your storefront using Site Builder.

To enable the app:

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Click Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application.
  3. Click Enable App.

The Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application includes the PowerReviews theme. Consult with your theme developer to extend the functionality of the PowerReviews theme to your site's theme. The functionality of this theme allows you to create a Write a Review page and use widgets to add PowerReviews content to your product pages.

To apply a theme:

  1. In Mozu Admin, click Site Builder.
  2. Hover over your site and select Theme.
  3. Hover over the theme that includes PowerReviews functionality and click Apply.

To add a Write a Review page:

  1. Click Site Builder to return to the Site Builder landing page.
  2. Hover over your site and select Edit.
  3. On the site map, click Single Pages»Add Page.
  4. Add a Write a Review page by entering Write a Review for the Page Title, accepting the default Page Url, and selecting Write a Review from the template drop-down.
  5. Click Save.

To add PowerReviews widgets to your Product page, refer to the Add Widgets to Your Storefront section of this guide.

Note: PowerReviews widgets may not display until after the first import of PowerReviews content. To enable PowerReviews widgets on your Category pages, consult with your theme developer.

Add Widgets to Your Storefront

Shoppers interact with PowerReviews content on your storefront through widgets that you add to pages. The following PowerReviews widgets are available in Site Builder for use on the Product page:

  • Review Snippet—Allows customers to enter reviews.
  • Q&A Snippet—Allows customers to ask questions.
  • Review Display—Displays reviews.
  • Q&A Display—Displays questions and answers.
  • PowerReviews ROI Beacon—Measures how product reviews and Q&A affect sale conversions. For use on your site's checkout page.

To add a widget, you may need help with customized code for adding dropzones or JavaScript code. Your theme developer or Mozu staff can help you achieve these tasks.

  1. In Mozu Admin, select Site Builder.
  2. Hover over your theme and select Edit.
  3. In the list of pages, expand the Templates section and select Product.
    Note: To use the PowerReviews ROI Beacon widget, select the Checkout page.
  4. Enable View dropzones.
  5. Click Widgets and drag PowerReviews Product Reviews or PowerReviews ROI Beacon into a dropzone.
  6. Choose a widget type in the configuration window.
    If you want shoppers to see the review and Q&A sections in separate tabs, enable Is Tab Enabled? and use Tab Code to enter the ID of the div that contains the tab. Note: Your theme must support the tab for for the tabbed display to work. Consult with your theme developer if you have questions about enabling and identifying the tab in your theme.
  7. Click Save.

Import and Export Data

Before importing and exporting data, ensure you have configured and enabled the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application.

Export Data

You can export reviews data about your products and categories from Mozu to PowerReviews using the Catalog or Order pages in Mozu Admin. Mozu recommends that you initially perform a manual export to check whether any errors occur during the export process.

  1. In Mozu Admin, click Catalog or Orders.
  2. Click Extensions > PowerReviews > Export.
  3. Click Export Now.

If you encounter any errors, click on the error number in the list to identify the category or product that is causing the error. After the first manual export, Mozu automatically exports data if you enabled scheduled exports during configuration. Exports occur once a day between 2 and 3 am CST. You can view your exports through your PowerReviews account.

Note: Mozu recommends specifying a brand for your products when using the export feature of the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application. PowerReviews uses the brand attribute when it mentions products in emails and reviews. If you don't specify a brand attribute (optional in Mozu), then PowerReviews displays blank information where otherwise it would display the brand. You can specify a brand for a product using the Catalog module. Open the Products editor and fill in the Brand field under Properties.

Import Data

You can import reviews data for your products using the configuration page for the Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application:

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Click Mozu PowerReviews Enterprise Application.
  3. Click the Configuration link to open configuration settings.
  4. On the Import tab, click Import Now.

After you import data for the first time, Mozu automatically imports data based on the settings you set during configuration. For example, if you configure your settings at 10 am to import data every four hours, the imports occur six times a day at 2 am, 6 am, 10 am, 2 pm, 6 pm, and 10 pm. To view imported ratings for each product, use the Catalog page in Mozu Admin (select Catalog > Inventory and search for a product). A Rating field displays in the Properties section, showing a calculated average of all PowerReview shopper ratings entered through your storefront, or syndicated from external sites if your PowerReview account provides syndication services. The PowerReviews storefront theme uses the Rating property as one option to facet and filter products on your storefront.

Congratulations! You can now preview PowerReviews functionality on your storefront.
