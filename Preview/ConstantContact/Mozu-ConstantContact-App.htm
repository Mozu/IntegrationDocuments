Welcome to Constant Contact and Mozu

Dev Center Version: 1.0.0
App Version: 1.19.0

Constant Contact is a leading provider of email marketing services, offering easy-to-use templates, streamlined contact management, and robust campaign analytics. The Constant Contact Application by Mozu integrates Constant Contact as your email marketing provider, sharing contact information between the two systems and enabling you to map customer data to Constant Contact customer attributes.

Application Features:

  • Synchronizes contact information and opt-in preferences across Mozu and Constant Contact.
  • Maps customer name, email, and address to Constant Contact customer attributes.
  • (Optional) Enables Constant Contact to send automated welcome emails to new customers.

Install the App

You can install the Constant Contact Application by Mozu directly from the Mozu App Marketplace:

  1. Go to: www.mozu.com/marketplace
  2. In the Search field, enter: Constant Contact
  3. Click the app icon.
  4. On the app page, click Install Now.

Note: The Constant Contact Application installs and functions on the tenant level. It processes customer marketing updates for all sites on your tenant.

Configure the App

To integrate Constant Contact with Mozu, review the configuration requirements to ensure you have everything you need to be successful before you begin, and then complete the steps in this section.

Configuration Requirements

  • You must have an active Constant Contact account.
  • The Constant Contact Application by Mozu must be installed on your tenant.

Open the App Configuration Settings

  1. Log in to Mozu Admin.
  2. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  3. Click Constant Contact Application by Mozu.
  4. Click the Configuration link to open configuration settings.

Enable Constant Contact Access

For the app to successfully communicate with Constant Contact, you must allow Mozu access to your Constant Contact account:

  1. In the Mozu app configuration settings dialog, go to the Info tab.
  2. Click Login to Constant Contact.
  3. Enter your user name and password.
  4. On the Allow Access? page, click Allow.

Configure Your Contact List and Welcome Email Settings

  1. In the Mozu app configuration settings dialog, go to the Settings tab.
  2. Enter a name for your Mozu contact list under Contact List Name. This field supports type-ahead completion for any existing lists in your Constant Contact account, or you can create a new list.
  3. (Optional) If you want new customers to receive your Constant Contact welcome email when they opt-in to marketing from your Mozu site, select Send welcome email to customer on signup.Note: If you are using Constant Contact for your welcome email, you must disable the default transactional email set on your Mozu site. Go to the General Settings page in Mozu Admin and disable the Welcome option under Notifications > Enable Mozu Transactional Emails > Shoppers.
  4. Select the email address you want to use for your emails in the Specify From and ReplyTo email for Campaigns drop-down.
  5. Click Save. If you created a new contact list, that list now appears as an empty list in Constant Contact.

Export Your Mozu Contacts to Constant Contact

Before you start creating your email campaigns, you must do an initial export of your customers who have opted in to marketing.

Note: All your new customer entries must originate from Mozu. You cannot create a new customer account from Constant Contact.

  1. In the Mozu app configuration settings dialog, go to the Sync tab.
  2. Select To Constant Contact.
  3. Click Sync Contacts. The job starts running immediately.
  4. Go to the Sync Logs tab and click Refresh. The job should appear in the list with a Status of RUNNING.
  5. Wait a few moments and click Refresh again. The job now shows a Status of COMPLETE, and the Total Contacts column shows the number of contacts that were updated.
  6. (Optional) If any errors occur with the sync, click the number in the Errors column to view the error messages. The most common error is an invalidly formatted email address.

Enable the App

After configuration, enable the Constant Contact Application in Mozu to apply its functionality to your tenant.

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Click Constant Contact Application by Mozu.
  3. Click Enable App.

Congratulations! You can now preview Constant Contact functionality on your tenant.

Use the App

Once you have installed, configured, and enabled the Constant Contact Application, the app handles all synchronization of contact info without additional action on your part. However, you can always use the configuration settings dialog to check the status of syncs between the two systems.

View Customer Update Events from Mozu

The Constant Contact app works by listening for events that fire when a customer account is updated in Mozu. The app recognizes both updates you make in Mozu Admin and updates that customers initiate from your Mozu storefront when they create a new account and opt in to marketing.

The Constant Contact app syncs Mozu events to Constant Contact every 30 minutes. You can view the event queue in the app configuration settings dialog:

  1. In the Mozu app configuration settings dialog, go to the Events tab.
  2. If the list is empty, click Refresh. Any queued events appear in the events grid.Tip: The Object Id in the event queue is the Mozu customer account ID for the customer record that triggered the event.
  3. When the run time indicated at the top of the tab has passed, click Refresh again. The list should empty to indicate that the event has been processed.

View Sync Logs from Constant Contact

The Constant Contact Application polls Constant Contact every 30 minutes to check if a customer has unsubscribed from a mailing list. If a customer does unsubscribe, the app updates their Mozu customer account to indicate they no longer opt in to marketing from your store.

Note: If a customer is on multiple mailing lists in Constant Contact and only requests to unsubscribe from a specific list, the app will still propagate the opt-out request to the customer account in Mozu. However, this change will not get pushed back to Constant Contact for any mailing list that is not actively selected on the Settings tab of the app configuration dialog.

You can view logs of each sync operation on the Sync tab:

  1. In the Mozu app configuration settings dialog, go to the Sync tab.
  2. Click From Constant Contact.
  3. In the list of sync operations, click View in the Logs column to see the types of events that were captured in the last sync.

The following section gives further detail on how the app handles updates made in Constant Contact.

App Logic

Updates from Mozu

The Constant Contact app works by listening for events that fire when a customer account is updated in Mozu. The app recognizes both updates you make in Mozu Admin and updates that customers initiate by creating a new account and opting into marketing.

The Constant Contact app syncs the two systems every 30 minutes.

Updates from Constant Contact

The Constant Contact app only checks to see if a customer has unsubscribed from a mailing list. To avoid potential issues with order fulfillment, it does not process any other changes made to a contact in Constant Contact. If you need to make a modification to a customer's account information, it is a best practice to only do so from Mozu Admin.

If a customer updates their own contact info in Constant Contact, such as by updating their email address, any emails you send from Constant Contact will go to the appropriate address. Mozu will not overwrite this change unless you force a new To Constant Contact sync from the Sync tab.

If you do force a new To Constant Contact sync for a mailing list on which a customer has updated their contact info, that customer will appear twice on that mailing list - once with the old contact info and once with the new info. For this reason, Mozu recommends avoiding a forced sync to Constant Contact after initial app set-up.
