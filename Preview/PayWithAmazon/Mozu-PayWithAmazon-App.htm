Welcome to Pay with Amazon and Mozu

Amazon Payments is a service that provides you with the ability to send payments for goods or services by using the payment methods already associated with your Amazon.com account. To make a payment, you can use a credit card, bank account, or Amazon Payments Account balance.

The Pay with Amazon by Mozu application integrates the Amazon Payments service with the Mozu platform, making Pay with Amazon a checkout option for shoppers.

Install Pay with Amazon by Mozu Application on Your Tenant

Call your Mozu sales or professional services representative and request installation on your tenant. Mozu staff will install the app to your tenant, making it available for further configuration and usage in your storefront.

Update Theme

To enable Pay with Amazon on your storefront, you must configure your Amazon Payments account information in the Payment Settings page in Mozu Admin. Once this information has been entered, the Pay with Amazon service is available, but in order for it to appear on your storefront for your shoppers your theme must be updated.

To update your theme to support Pay with Amazon, review and apply the changes made in our sample implementation to your own theme.

Configure the Application in Mozu Admin

Now that your Pay with Amazon application is installed, go to your Mozu Admin tools to configure the integration.

  1. Go to Settings > Payment & Checkout.
  2. Check the PayByAmazon checkbox. Once checked, the various configuration fields appear.
  3. Select the Amazon environment you wish to use. Note that this selection has nothing to do with Mozu production or sandbox environments; this option selects the Amazon environment.
    • Select Sandbox if you wish to use Amazon’s sandbox environment for testing your Pay with Amazon integration.
    • Select Production to use a working Amazon environment that processes payments.
  4. Obtain the below values from Amazon Seller Central (https://sellercentral.amazon.com/):
    • Seller ID
    • Client ID
    • Applicaton ID
    • AWS Access Key
    • AWS Secret
  5. Select an AWS region.
  6. Select an order processing method:
    • Authorize and Capture on Order Placement: This method authorizes and captures payment when an order is placed.
    • Authorize on Order Placement and Capture on Order Shipment: This method authorizes a payment when an order is placed, but does not capture that payment until the order has shipped.
  7. Click Save to save your Pay with Amazon settings.
