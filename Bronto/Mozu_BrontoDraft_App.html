Welcome to Bronto and Mozu

Bronto provides email marketing services and distribution. With an advanced marketing automation engine and solutions for shopping cart abandonment, post-purchase campaigns and powerful e-commerce integrations, Bronto is the premier cloud-based marketing platform for commerce.

Mozu offers an app to leverage Bronto's services to integrate with your Mozu customer list and subscribed shoppers. Through your Mozu app, you can sync your Bronto mailing lists with Mozu subscribers, build email campaigns in Bronto, and generate emails using your subscribed mailing list customers in your Mozu store.

Install Bronto Integration by Mozu on Your Tenant

After purchasing the Bronto Application by Mozu, call your sales or professional services representative and request installation on your tenant. Mozu staff will install the app to your tenant, making it available for further configuration and usage in your storefront.

This custom app integrates Bronto with Mozu to support the following:

  • Sync your Bronto account with Mozu to add current subscribers to your Bronto mailing list. All additional and removed subscribers automatically sync between Mozu and Bronto.
  • Export and import Bronto contacts and Mozu customers between your services.
  • Create and send email campaigns in Bronto to your Mozu customers.
  • Review a history of data syncs between Mozu and Bronto, automatic and manual.

When an email campaign is built, the following occurs between Mozu and the Bronto app:

  • You only need to sync Mozu and Bronto once to add subscribed customers between Mozu and Bronto. Additional syncs may be needed in case of Bronto maintenance or server outages to verify all subscribers.
  • As shoppers select to join the mailing list by subscribing, or click the unsubscribe link, the customer settings in Mozu and contacts in Bronto automatically update and sync.
  • Mozu provides a history of sync jobs, automatic and manual.

Configuration Requirements

To configure Bronto to work seamlessly with Mozu, you need specific items installed and settings provided. This guide details each step from requirements to final configuration for syncing contacts and generating email campaigns.

You must meet the following criteria to configure the Bronto app:

  • You must have the Bronto Application by Mozu installed into your tenant.
  • You must have an active Bronto account with an API Key. When added, the available list and site names on your account display in the app.
  • You must maintain all campaign designs, rules, and settings in your Bronto account.

Initialize and Enable the Bronto App

Prior to creating campaigns and sending emails, you must initialize then enable the Bronto app.

Configure Bronto Account Settings

To initialize the app, you add the API Key and link one site to one list. Mozu only supports one site per tenant at this time.

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Double-click Bronto.
  3. Click the Configuration link to open configuration settings.
  4. On the Settings tab, enter your Bronto API Key. If you need help finding your API Key, see the next section Find the Bronto API Key. When you click outside of the field, Mozu contacts Bronto to establish the connection. On success, a green checkmark displays. When selected, click out of the list to save the change.
  5. Data of available lists fills the List Name drop-down menu.
  6. Select a List Name to associate between Mozu and Bronto. Mozu only supports one site per tenant at this time.
  7. When selected, the association automatically verifies and displays.
    8. The app page updates displaying Initialized: true.

Find the Bronto API Key

If you are unsure where to find this key, do the following:

  1. Log in to your Bronto account online.
  2. Open the Account page and click Extras > API keys.
  3. A list of keys will display on the page. If not, click Create a Key and continue those steps.
  4. Copy and paste the long API Key into the Mozu App.

The API Key grants access to sync contacts and send email campaigns.

Enable the Bronto App

With the app initialized, you can enable the app:

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Double-click Bronto.
  3. Click Enable App on the Bronto page.

Configure the Application

To integrate your Bronto services with Mozu, you need to sync the data. The applications require at least one import and export to connect and update. Syncs occur constantly between the applications and services while the app is initialized and enabled. If you ever want to manually sync data, you can run these commands as needed.

Import Bronto Contacts

With your Mozu and Bronto accounts newly connected, you should import your Bronto contacts. These contacts add as new Mozu customers or update the subscription settings for current customers. Once synced, Bronto adds any current subscribers to Mozu customers. Any new or removed subscribers automatically update between Mozu and Bronto. Once synced, if a Bronto contact changes their status, it automatically syncs and updates to Mozu.

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Double-click Bronto.
  3. Click the Configuration link to open configuration settings.
  4. On the Customers tab, select the Synchronization Type as Import Bronto Customers.
  5. Click Schedule Sync. A job issues to update subscribed and unsubscribed customers with your contact list.
  6. A new record adds to the History tab.

Export Mozu Customers

With your Mozu and Bronto accounts newly connected, you should export your Mozu customers. These customer accounts add as new Bronto contacts or update the subscription settings for current contacts. Once synced, Mozu adds any current subscribers to Bronto contacts. Any new or removed subscribers automatically update between Mozu and Bronto. Once synced, if a Mozu customer changes their status, it automatically syncs and updates to Bronto.

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings > Applications.
  2. Double-click Bronto.
  3. Click the Configuration link to open configuration settings.
  4. On the Customers tab, select the Synchronization Type as Export Mozu Customers.
  5. Click Schedule Sync. A job issues to update subscribed and unsubscribed customers with your contact list.
  6. A new record adds to the History tab.
