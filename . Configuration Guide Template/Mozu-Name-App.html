Install the Application Name on Your Tenant

After purchasing the Application Name, call your sales or professional services representative and request installation on your tenant. Mozu staff will install the app to your tenant, making it available for configuration and usage in your storefront.

Configure the Application

To integrate your Partner Company services with Mozu, review the configuration requirements to ensure you have everything you need to be successful before you begin, and then complete the steps in this section.

Configuration Requirements

  • The Application Name must be installed on your tenant.
  • You must have an active Partner Company account.
  • (Example) You must have access to modify and update your storefront theme.

Enable the App

After configuration, enable the Application Name in Mozu to apply its functionality to your tenant:

  1. On the Mozu Admin top bar, select Settings»Applications.
  2. Click Application Name in Software.
  3. Click Enable App.
